You command a set of agents. Your goal is to kill a sniper hiding in the field. Click in the field to place an agent. The agent will kill the sniper if the sniper is in the agent's range. The sniper will kill any agent in its range. Once you have placed all your agents then the next agent you place will move one of the agents you previously placed (and is not dead) to the new position.

You win when you hit the sniper first.

You lose when all your guys are dead before you hit the sniper.
